Correct Peptide Reconstitution

Compounds are commonly delivered in a lyophilized state to preserve stability during storage and transport. Proper research handling helps maintain consistency, integrity, and reliable measurement.

This calculator is a measurement tool for research planning only. It converts vial amount, water volume, selected syringe size, and selected research dose into estimated syringe units.

How the Calculator Works

The calculator converts milligrams to micrograms, calculates concentration per mL, then converts the selected research amount into U-100 syringe units.

1 mL equals 100 units. A 0.3 mL syringe holds 30 units, a 0.5 mL syringe holds 50 units, and a 1.0 mL syringe holds 100 units.

Choose the syringe size being used. The main tube will rescale to 30, 50, or 100 units.
